Who we are ?

E-Multitech Solution was established in 2010 by two science and technology researchers, Utsav Subedi and Roshan Subedi. The company’s founder and Managing Director Roshan Subedi is an information technology expert who spends most of his time supervising and coordinating projects for his team. The software company, located in the busy streets of Kathmandu, Nepal, has developed and positioned itself as a leading provider of software solutions. Having completed hundreds of projects of varying levels of complexity, specialising in web development, web design, mobile applications, and business automation tools, E-Multitech Solution takes pride in providing user-friendly and cost-effective designs, web development and mobile apps to our clients all around the world.

Flexible Approach

With a careful and flexible approach, we've managed to complete every project we have undertaken. We love to involve you in the development phase, to ensure that you know exactly what is going on with the project. As a result we are able to redesign or make small changes along the way, and conserve valuable resources that can be applied elsewhere. We will listen to all sides of the argument in order to come up with an effective plan of execution before the project begins. Throughout the project development process, we will consider all alternatives before beginning each phase so that the project can proceed smoothly. We always welcome constructive criticism. But once we begin a development phase, we cannot and will not make changes until the phase completes and the next round begins.

Good Software Development

We make sure nothing is left out and everything runs exactly as you expect. Our team will give your project everything they have, because good web-development practice means going that extra mile to ensure that you get what you have asked for. Our priority is to complete the project on time, under budget, and according to what we have discussed in our contract with you. We will not move on to the next phase unless we are both 100% satisfied.
