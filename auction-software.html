Penny Auction As live Bidding

Penny Auction is a strategy game, where participants pay a non-refundable fee in order to place a small incremental bid - the auction price then increases by a small amount such as 0.01 or 0.06 cents. When the auction expires, the last participant to have placed a bid wins the item and also pays the final price, which is usually significantly lower than the actual price of the item.
