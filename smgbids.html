Our Features

1.

Auction Management enables efficient product auction management, bid history viewing, and facilitates name-based search and category-based filtering. Products can be added in single or multiple languages.

2.

Category Management allows for streamlined management of various product categories across multiple languages.

3.

Member Management provides oversight of registered users, their transaction history, bid history, and tracks user IP addresses.

4.

Winner Management empowers winners to track auctions, monitor payment status, and review shipped items.

5.

Order Management lists user-ordered products, enabling admins to review details, payment statuses, and ship items.

6.

Bid Package Management offers administrators the flexibility to set various bid package types.

7.

Voucher Management facilitates promotional discounts when purchasing bid packages.

8.

Language Management enables the enabling and disabling of website languages.

9.

Country Management allows for the management of different countries on the website, including language, currency, exchange rate, and time zone settings.
