Project Overview

uBiduBuy offers shoppers a fun and exciting way to buy their favourite luxury branded goods at a heavily discounted price. This is done by participating in either a “Lowest Unique Bid” auction or a “Price Reveal” auction. These are “pay to bid” auctions.At uBiduBuy They wish for their clients to enjoy the experience and excitement to participate and win in their auctions whilst at the same time give them the opportunity should they not win, the option to buy the item at it’s normal retail price. Upon purchase of the item uBiduBuy will return their normal bidding credits used in that auction back to them as bonus credits, on a 1 for 1 basis.
