Project Overview

Our Unique Bid auction platform offers an innovative bidding experience, allowing users to submit bids up to two decimal places. To win the "Lowest Unique Bid" auction, a valid lowest unique bid must be placed by the auction's conclusion. Our application boasts multi-currency and multi-language support, along with seamless integration of payment gateways such as PayTm, CCAvenue, and PayPal. Additionally, features like desktop notifications, push notifications, and more are incorporated into our platform, all constructed on the robust MVC framework.

Our Features

1.

Auction Management enables efficient product auction management, bid history viewing, and facilitates name-based search and category-based filtering. Products can be added in single or multiple languages.

2.

Category Management allows for streamlined management of various product categories across multiple languages.

Member Management provides oversight of registered users, their transaction history, bid history, and tracks user IP addresses.
